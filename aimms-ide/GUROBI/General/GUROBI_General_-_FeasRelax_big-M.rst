FeasRelax Big M
===============



:Type:	Floating point number	
:Range:	[0.0,1e100]	
:Default:	1e6	



When relaxing a constraint in a feasibility relaxation, it is sometimes necessary to introduce a big-M value. This option determines the default magnitude of that value.
