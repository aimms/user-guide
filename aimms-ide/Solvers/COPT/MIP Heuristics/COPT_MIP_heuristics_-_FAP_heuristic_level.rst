FAP heuristic level
===================



:Type:	Selection
:Range:	The settings listed below
:Default:	Automatic



This option controls the level of Fix-and-Propagate (FAP) heuristics applied during MIP solving. Possible values are:

    *	Automatic
    *	Off
    *	Fast
    *	Normal
    *	Aggressive
    *	Heavy
