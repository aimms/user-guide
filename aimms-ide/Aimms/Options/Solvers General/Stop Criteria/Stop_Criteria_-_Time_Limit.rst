Time Limit
==========



:param Type:	Integer	
:param Range:	{0 .. :ref:`Miscellaneous_Maxint`}	
:param Default:	:ref:`Miscellaneous_Maxint` 	



After Time Limit CPU seconds have elapsed, solution procedures are interrupted and the current solution is returned to AIMMS.
